Identity authentication

When creating a new Dapio account, we’ll ask you to verify yourself through one form of government-issued identity document. This helps us confirm the details you’ve provided and prevents identity fraud.

Pin passcode

After onboarding we will send you a PIN code to your registered email to keep your app secure.

Password protected

We will ask you to set up a secure password, this will be used to log into your seller dashboard, anytime, anywhere.
